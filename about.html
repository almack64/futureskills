About Allen Patrick

Allen Patrick is the visionary founder of Future Skills Training & Tutoring, a platform dedicated to empowering learners to excel in a world shaped by AI and automation. Combining extensive experience in education, engineering, and professional development, Allen delivers innovative solutions that prepare individuals to thrive in an ever-changing technological landscape.

A Mission for Future-Ready Learning

Allen’s mission is simple: to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI advancements and practical applications. Through Future Skills, he teaches learners how to collaborate with AI tools to enhance productivity, creativity, and problem-solving. His approach focuses on leveraging context, such as personal strengths, organizational goals, and industry-specific needs, to unlock AI’s full potential.

Expertise You Can Trust

As an experienced AI Agent Engineer, Allen has developed AI-driven solutions for workflow optimization, content creation, and automation. His technical expertise, backed by a foundation in mechanical engineering and certifications in FDA and ISO standards, ensures a precise and impactful approach to teaching AI applications. He’s helped professionals and organizations streamline processes, save time, and innovate for the future.

Innovative Educational Programs

  • AI-Focused High School Tutoring: Introducing ethical AI use in math and science to build confidence and skills.
  • Professional AI Consulting: Providing hands-on guidance for individuals seeking to remain competitive in their careers.
  • "AI & Your Career" Program: A cohort-based curriculum designed to take learners from foundational knowledge to expert application in real-world scenarios.

A Legacy of Leadership

Allen’s passion for teaching and innovation shines in every aspect of Future Skills. He has redefined how learners interact with AI tools like Google NotebookLM, ChatGPT, DALL-E, and Microsoft Designer, ensuring they gain not only knowledge but also practical experience to lead in their fields.

With Future Skills, Allen ensures you’re not just keeping up with AI advancements—you’re leading the way.
