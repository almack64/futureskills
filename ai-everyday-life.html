AI for Everyday Life: A Beginner’s Guide

A live, interactive class for AI beginners aged 50+

Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming the world, yet many are unsure how to integrate it into daily life. This course is designed especially for older adults who want to learn how to harness AI for practical, everyday tasks. In a small-group, live session, you’ll receive a hands-on introduction to AI tools that simplify your daily routines and make technology more accessible.

Who Is This For?

  • Individuals aged 50+ who are comfortable with basic technology (smartphone, email, internet) but new to AI.
  • Those who want to understand AI in simple, practical terms.
  • People seeking affordable, guided instruction with interactive, hands-on activities.
  • Anyone looking to use AI to stay connected with family, improve daily tasks, and remain informed in the digital age.

What You’ll Learn

  • ✓ Understand the basics of AI and how it works.
  • ✓ Use ChatGPT for everyday tasks such as drafting emails, setting reminders, or learning new skills.
  • ✓ Explore AI-powered tools like voice assistants (Alexa, Siri), AI-generated images (DALL-E), and auto-transcription apps.
  • ✓ Recognize AI scams and learn tips to stay safe online.
  • ✓ Build confidence in using AI to communicate across generations.

Course Format

  • Duration: 1.5 hours per session
  • Class Size: 5-10 participants
  • Platform: Google Meet or Zoom
  • Cost: $25 per session
  • Materials: PDF takeaway guide with step-by-step instructions

Session Highlights

Each session is designed to be engaging and interactive, featuring live demonstrations, hands-on activities, and open discussions. The structure may vary to best meet the needs of the group.
